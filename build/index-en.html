Play responsibly with FAVBET.

The Safebox is a feature designed to help you manage your spending by temporarily locking a portion of your balance, making it unavailable for gameplay.

To "Safebox"

How to use the safebox?

Make sure:

You are a verified player.

You have funds on your main balance.

Set a limit:

  • Go to
  • Personal Office
  • Balance
  • Safebox

Enter the amount you want to restrict. This amount will be unavailable for gameplay.

Withdraw funds:

  • In the
  • Safebox
  • section, click
  • “Transfer  Out”

Choose the amount you want to unlock and return to your main balance. The funds will become available for gameplay.
